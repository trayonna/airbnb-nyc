New York City is known for its restricted living spaces, but New Yorkers who rent out their rooms on AirBnB are doing everything they can to shift attention from that.

A text analysis of nearly 50,000 AirBnB listing names across the five boroughs shows that the words “ cozy,” “ private,” and “ spacious” are frequently used to describe rooms and apartments.

But each borough returned a different set of adjectives. Sure, “ brownstone ” makes sense for Brooklyn, but what about “convenient” to describe a place in Staten Island? We’re not as convinced.
